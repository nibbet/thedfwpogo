# GO Wild Area: Global 2025

## Event Overview

Get ready for the next major Pokemon GO event coming to trainers worldwide!

## Event Details

**Dates:** March 15-16, 2025
**Location:** Global Event (Play from anywhere!)
**Cost:** Free to play, with optional ticket for $9.99 USD

## What to Expect

- **Increased Spawns:** Rare Pokemon appearing more frequently
- **Special Research:** Exclusive storyline and encounters
- **Exclusive Moves:** Event-exclusive moves for certain Pokemon
- **Shiny Releases:** New shiny Pokemon debuts
- **Global Challenges:** Work together with trainers worldwide

## Ticket Benefits

- Exclusive Special Research
- Bonus encounters with rare Pokemon
- Additional raid passes
- Exclusive avatar items
- Extended event features
